AWESOME FIRMS - CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM

Awesome Firms, an IIM alumni venture, is a leadership development platform that helps ambitious high performers become future leaders in their chosen areas.

Apply for Student Ambassador :

The Awesome Firms student Ambassador Program recognizes hard-working, motivated and enthusiastic individuals by giving them the opportunity to represent Awesome Firms on their university campus. Awesome Firms Student Ambassadors are current students who act as a link between the University and the outside world. They use their own experiences to give people in University life an idea of what it is like to be associate oneself beyond the university life and gives a sneak peek into the world of industry at larger view.

Student Ambassadors play a vital support role such as:

• Leading & representing the college campus to the industry, and shoulder the responsibility of making this planet a better and a smarter place!

• Sharing their experiences with students and motivating them to be part of the venture on various activities. This program also provides the Student Ambassador with an excellent opportunity to gain valuable work experience and sharpen your skills such as communication, collaboration, team work and working with specialists in the field of Leadership Development, we feel in today's collaborative environment, there needs to be a platform where like minds can come together on a common forum to discuss, debate, and exchange ideas on various tools and strategies.



What Awesome Firms has planned for you?

• Develop valuable leadership, communication and project management skills.

• Start to grow your professional network.

• See your creativity, innovation and BIG ideas come to fruition on campus.

• Be an integral part of campus spirit, learning and information-sharing.

• A opportunity to work closely with a person having 22 years of experience in the field of entrepreneurship and start-up and explore it’s depths.

• Many special discounts and facilities on our product and seminars.



What about Prerequisites?

• Second to final year student.

• Strong interpersonal skills.

• Involvement in campus life (extracurricular activities, student organizations).
• ‏Passion for learning, creativity, innovation and information-sharing.

• A basic knowledge about leadership development (what is leadership, how it influences lives).

Students having strong leadership and immense love for entrepreneurship and business, are most welcome to be a part of our extended family on their university.
